Tutorial experience requirements
For each unit, create a tutorial that:
- Explains concepts very clearly in a step-by-step way
- Breaks down ideas into manageable learning steps
- Lets the student move forward, go back, revisit any step, or skip to a specific step
- Shows a clear map or outline of the tutorial flow so the student always knows where they are
- Uses language that is age-appropriate for a 9th grade Honors Geometry student
- Prioritizes conceptual understanding, not just memorization
- Includes diagrams or visual explanations where helpful
- Connects ideas clearly to classwork and likely test expectations

Learning content requirements
For each unit, include:
- Concept lessons
- Worked examples
- Guided practice
- Independent practice
- Challenge problems
- Short checks for understanding
- Practice quizzes or mini-tests throughout the unit

- One easy-reference exam summary for quick review before a big test
- The summary should be short, high value, and easy to scan on test day
